Cybersecurity

What is cybersecurity?

Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It's also known as information technology security or electronic information security. The term applies in a variety of contexts, from business to mobile computing, and can be divided into a few common categories. Two Categories that are part of cybersecurity are listed below.

Network Security

Network Security Is the practice of security of a computer network from intruders, whether targeted attackers or opportunistic malware. Network security consists of the policies and practices adopted to prevent and monitor unauthorized access, misuse, modification, or denial of a computer network and network-accessible resources. Network security involves the authorization of access to data in a network, which is controlled by the network administrator.

Application Security

Application Security Is focuses on keeping software and devices free of threats. A compromised application could provide access to the data its designed to protect. Successful security begins in the design stage, well before a program or device is deployed. This includes the work administrators and application security engineers do to better understand why applications expose vulnerabilities to exploitations in security and how to make them safer in the future.

How cybersecurity has evolved.

Cybersecurity has evolved repeatedly in the past decade according to the founder of CYBERSAFE. It was not a big part of the IT infrastructure till the hackers got smarter over the years and it got easy to get personal and business information. Cyber terrorism is known to widespread fear in computer more like malware. That can be used to get important information without business knowing. It can also damage the computers to make money or even have a political motivation behind it. 👮

One of the ways that cybersecurity has evolved is that before we could use any password for our Gmail, Facebook, twitter etc. But wouldn’t know if it was strong enough for anyone else to guess it, now the apps give some sort of error or message that tells us its either not long enough or it’s too easy to guess or it’s common. There are some apps that gives an option to turn on 2 step verification, which means even if someone guessed your password the system askes for a verification code which gets send to the user’s phone number or selected email address sometimes even an app that user selects to help with verifications. All these new systems show how cybersecurity has evolved in the past 5-10 years.

Impact of Cybersecurity and Job Opportunities

Potential impact of this development - With new systems and software developing to help securing business and personal computer files and information it has helped many people in daily computer tasks. From securing your account passwords to securing major file that are important to an organization, cyber security plays a big role in this era as there are many trojans and malware around. When talking about having such a security it also important to talk about someone who takes the responsibility of making sure this security works the way it’s designed for.  Since this development has been a such rise in the IT industry it has also created a new job know as Security Analysts, their job is to design, and implanting security measures while considering organizations requirements. They are also to properly configure and update security software’s and creating a plan of action in case a security breach happens.

Security Analysts are not always behind a computer or updating software’s, there are less technical sides such as updating employees on security protocols, working to prevent type of attacked called “Social Engineering”- these attacks rely more on human error rather than technical errors. According to the Bureau of labor Statistics most security Analysts work in the computer industry, others are working in finance and insurance, company management or administrative and support services. With having flexibility of working from home or at the office, the analysts are also required to be on call at nights or weekends in case there are after office hours security breach.

With the service of cyber security the most effected people are the business owners and the high-end officials. Business owners are affected in a good way because they can now stop worrying about data being easily hacked into. People owning big companies are in constant worries about their data security. With the help of these software’s at least they can cross that little bridge with ease.
